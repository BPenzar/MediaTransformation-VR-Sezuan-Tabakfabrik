Mediatransformation I.

Here we try to translate a scene of Brecht's theater performance "Der gute Mensch von Sezuan" into a
Web-VR-Application

The play as TV-Production: Der gute Mensch von Sezuan

Tabakfabrik (S.111-118)

The chosen scene will show workers in a tobacco fabric. At first, the viewpoint will be the one of a worker. Later on the viewpoint will change to a foreman's one.

Concept

A general concept of our VR application as .key document (in german): Praesentation: Brecht's Tabakfabrik
